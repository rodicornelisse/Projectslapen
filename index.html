bubo in a nutshell

bubo is a personal sleep assistant to help your patient fall asleep. bubo tells pre-recorded stories from their parents or tells one of his own adventures. This will create a comfortable, stress-free environment and helps to maintain the parent-child relationship.

Why bubo?

A sleep companion

Parents can provide comfort, and can, as no other, help their child to fall asleep. However, parents can not always be there, especially during night-time. Due to background noises, stress, and being woken up regularly for measurements, hospitals often fail to prevent insomnia. With bubo, parents stay connected with their child.

Helps the recovery

To help the recovery, children need to get their recommended amount of sleep. Since hospitals often fail to do this, the recovery will be inhibited, prolonging the admission in the hospital. foursleep group wants to help against insomnia for children in hospitals and maintain the parent-child relationship.

How it works

Record new stories

Parents can record new stories by using the bubo App. Parents can choose to record a story from the bubo App itself or record their own stories.

Maintain the parent-child relationship

Pre-recorded stories from their parents will be sent to bubo via WiFi. bubo will download the story automatically.

bubo story book

Once the story has been downloaded the book will turn from orange to blue. bubo story book works with touch controls.
